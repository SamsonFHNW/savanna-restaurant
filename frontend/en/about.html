Family & cuisine

Our story

Savanna was born from a wish to share, here in Delémont, the flavours that shaped our childhood in Eritrea and Ethiopia. Every dish carries a memory: a cuisine passed from mother to daughter, from feast to feast.

"At our table, no one eats from their own plate. We break the injera together."

Our spices come straight from home. The injera is made fresh every morning, sourdough-style, and every wat simmers for hours until it finds its balance. Nothing is rushed — that is what makes the difference.

The house is family-run. You may be welcomed by one of us, served by another, and leave — we hope — already wanting to come back.
